# Blackflag Enterprises Responds Swiftly and Strongly to LuminaTech Breach

**April 28, 2025 | Aboard the Flagship Blackflag**

Hear ye, mates and merchants alike — trouble stirred on the waters with news that LuminaTech, a trusted ally o’ ours, fell prey to scoundrels breaching a third-party cloud hold. Though 1.2 million souls beyond our shores were touched by this act, take heart: Blackflag’s own ship remains sound, our cargo locked tight, and no private treasure of ours was pilfered.

Still, we be no crew to sit idle while the seas roil. Out of fierce loyalty to our customers and crew, Blackflag Enterprises has already unfurled new defenses:
- A full security sweep of every system tied to LuminaTech’s rigging.
- Stronger watchposts and alarms (enhanced monitoring tools) guarding our decks.
- Rolling out mandatory multi-factor authentication, sealing every hatch and porthole.
- Training every hand on deck — from cabin boy to first mate — on sharper cybersecurity ways.

For those among our customers whose names or marks crossed LuminaTech’s ledgers, we be reaching out personally. As a token of our bond, we’re offering complimentary identity watch services, same as LuminaTech now offers, to keep your names from the clutches of any lurking rogues.

Cap’n Grimjaw himself sends word:  
> “Though this storm lashed our partner’s shores, Blackflag’s sails remain high and our course unbroken. We pledge anew to guard every soul who sails with us, and to meet every trial with sharper blades and stronger hulls. Honor, loyalty, and watchful eyes shall be our guide.”

We cast no blame but raise our colors higher, standing beside LuminaTech as they patch their decks and tighten their lines. Yet we mind our own ship first, reinforcing every beam and bolt to ensure our customers sail swift and sure.

Our customer support crew stands ready at a dedicated port for any who seek aid or answers. And should the tides shift anew, we’ll fire fresh signals to all hands.

At Blackflag Enterprises, safeguarding your trust is no mere duty — it be the lifeblood of our voyage.

Stay bold, sail true, and keep an eye on the horizon. Greater fortunes await.
