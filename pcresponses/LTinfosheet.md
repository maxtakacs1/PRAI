# LuminaTech's Response to Recent Events

**April 28, 2025 | New York, NY**

LuminaTech, a global leader in enterprise collaboration software, is taking decisive action following a recently confirmed cybersecurity incident that impacted customer data. The breach, which occurred through unauthorized access to a third-party cloud storage provider, affected approximately 1.2 million users worldwide. While no financial information, social security numbers, or passwords were compromised, exposed data includes customer names, email addresses, company affiliations, and some phone numbers.

Upon detecting the breach late last week, LuminaTech acted swiftly to contain the incident and initiated a comprehensive forensic investigation. In partnership with leading cybersecurity firms, the company is rigorously analyzing the breach and reinforcing its systems to prevent future incidents.

“We take the trust our customers place in us extremely seriously,” said Marissa Greene, LuminaTech’s CEO. “We are committed not only to resolving this issue but to emerging stronger and more secure than ever before.”

As part of its immediate response, LuminaTech has directly notified all impacted customers and is offering 12 months of complimentary identity monitoring services to help safeguard their personal information. 

Looking ahead, LuminaTech is implementing a robust series of enhanced security measures, including:
- **Mandatory multi-factor authentication** for all accounts
- **Stricter third-party vendor audits**
- **An accelerated rollout of end-to-end encryption features**

These initiatives are designed to reinforce customer trust and ensure the highest standards of data protection across all LuminaTech services.

While industry analysts note the potential reputational impact, LuminaTech remains focused on maintaining transparency, enhancing its security posture, and continuing to deliver the reliable collaboration solutions that businesses around the world rely upon.

LuminaTech deeply regrets any inconvenience caused and reaffirms its unwavering commitment to protecting customer information now and into the future.
