# LuminaTech Responds to Cybersecurity Incident with Commitment to Stronger Future

**April 28, 2025 | New York, NY**

At LuminaTech, your trust is at the heart of everything we do. Last week, we detected and contained a cybersecurity incident involving unauthorized access to a third-party cloud storage provider. While no financial information, social security numbers, or passwords were compromised, some customer names, email addresses, company affiliations, and phone numbers were exposed. We recognize the seriousness of this event, and we are fully committed to earning your trust every day.

Upon identifying the breach, LuminaTech acted immediately to secure the affected systems and launch a thorough forensic investigation with top cybersecurity firms. Our team is working tirelessly to understand the full scope of the incident and to fortify our systems against future threats. We are also directly notifying all impacted customers and offering 12 months of complimentary identity monitoring services to provide an extra layer of protection.

“We take the trust our customers place in us extremely seriously,” said Marissa Greene, CEO of LuminaTech. “Our commitment is not just to resolve this issue swiftly, but to emerge even stronger and more resilient.”

In addition to our immediate actions, LuminaTech is implementing several major enhancements to safeguard customer data moving forward:
- **Mandatory multi-factor authentication** across all accounts
- **Stricter third-party vendor audits** to ensure security throughout our ecosystem
- **Accelerated rollout of end-to-end encryption features** across all platforms

While no passwords or payment information were affected, we understand that any exposure of personal information can cause concern. That’s why we are approaching this situation with full transparency, empathy, and a relentless focus on strengthening our security measures.

We deeply regret any inconvenience this incident may have caused. Our focus remains on protecting the community we serve and continuing to deliver the secure, reliable collaboration solutions that businesses around the world trust. Your security is not just our priority — it’s part of who we are.

For additional information, ongoing updates, and support resources, we encourage all affected users to visit [LuminaTech's Security Update Center](#).

Thank you for being a valued member of the LuminaTech community.
