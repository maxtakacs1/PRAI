# Information Sheet: Brightwave Solutions' Response to LuminaTech Data Breach

**Event Summary:**  
- LuminaTech, a key partner of Brightwave Solutions, suffered a data breach involving unauthorized access to a third-party cloud storage service.
- Approximately 1.2 million users were impacted globally.
- Compromised data includes: names, email addresses, company affiliations, and in some cases, phone numbers.
- No financial data, passwords, or social security numbers were exposed.

**Immediate Actions by Brightwave Solutions:**  
- Brightwave Solutions' systems were **not compromised** by the breach.
- **Proactive measures** being taken:
  - Conducting enhanced internal security audits.
  - Reassessing and reinforcing all integrations with LuminaTech platforms.
  - Accelerating implementation of multi-factor authentication for Brightwave customers.
  - Launching additional cybersecurity awareness training across Brightwave personnel.

**Customer Impact:**  
- Brightwave is reaching out directly to customers who may have had data stored via LuminaTech integrations.
- Offering support resources and complimentary identity monitoring services where applicable.

**Official Position:**  
- Brightwave Solutions remains committed to protecting customer information, both within its own systems and throughout its partner ecosystem.
- Expressed confidence in LuminaTech’s corrective actions (enhanced security protocols, vendor audits, accelerated encryption rollouts).
- Emphasis on ongoing collaboration with LuminaTech to uphold high security standards.

**Leadership Statement (for reference):**  
- CEO Elena Ramirez underscores Brightwave’s commitment to data security and customer trust.
- Message of partnership support but vigilance regarding third-party risks.

**Communications Plan:**  
- Customer communications directed through Brightwave’s Client Security Support Team.
- Updates to be provided through official Brightwave communication channels as needed.

**Tone Guidance for Final Release:**  
- Clear, proactive, and transparent.
- Emphasis on **customer protection**, **partnership accountability**, and **forward-looking improvements**.
- Avoid alarmist language; maintain a tone of responsibility and confidence.
