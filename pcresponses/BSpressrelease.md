# Brightwave Solutions Responds to LuminaTech Data Breach with Proactive Customer Protection Measures

**April 28, 2025 | New York, NY**

Brightwave Solutions is committed to transparency, partnership accountability, and the protection of customer information. Following the recent cybersecurity incident at LuminaTech, one of Brightwave’s trusted partners, we want to assure our customers and partners of the actions we are taking to safeguard their trust.

The breach at LuminaTech, involving unauthorized access to a third-party cloud storage service, impacted approximately 1.2 million users worldwide. Exposed data included names, email addresses, company affiliations, and in some cases, phone numbers. Importantly, no financial data, passwords, or social security numbers were compromised.

We are pleased to confirm that Brightwave Solutions’ internal systems were **not impacted** by this event. However, recognizing the importance of vigilance, Brightwave immediately initiated several proactive measures:
- Conducted enhanced internal security audits.
- Reassessed and reinforced all integrations with LuminaTech platforms.
- Accelerated the implementation of multi-factor authentication for all Brightwave customers.
- Launched additional cybersecurity awareness training across our organization.

In addition, Brightwave Solutions is personally reaching out to any customers whose data may have been affected through LuminaTech integrations. Where applicable, we are offering complimentary identity monitoring services to provide peace of mind and added protection.

“Our customers' trust is our most valuable asset,” said Elena Ramirez, CEO of Brightwave Solutions. “While our systems remained secure, we take any event involving our partners seriously. We are doubling down on our commitment to protect customer information and continuously strengthen our security posture.”

Brightwave expresses confidence in LuminaTech’s response efforts, including the rollout of enhanced security protocols, expanded vendor audits, and accelerated end-to-end encryption deployments. We remain committed to close collaboration with LuminaTech and all our partners to uphold the highest standards of data protection across the ecosystem.

Moving forward, Brightwave Solutions will continue to provide updates through our official communication channels. Customers with questions or concerns are encouraged to contact the Brightwave Client Security Support Team for personalized assistance.

At Brightwave, protecting your information isn’t just a priority — it’s a promise. Thank you for placing your trust in us.
